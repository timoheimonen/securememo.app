About securememo.app

securememo.app lets you send short, private notes that vanish after reading. Everything is encrypted in your browser, so the server never sees what you wrote.

What makes it private

Encrypted in your browser

Your memo is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before it leaves your device. We only store ciphertext.

Passwords stay on your device

The encryption password is generated in your browser. It's never sent to or stored on our servers.

Memos delete themselves

A memo is removed after it's read, or when its expiry time runs out. Choose from 8 hours up to 30 days.

No accounts, no tracking

You don't need to sign up. We don't use cookies, analytics, or ads. We only keep the minimum data needed to run the service.

How to share a memo

  1. 1

    Write it

    Type your note and pick an expiry time. Your browser encrypts it before uploading.

  2. 2

    Share separately

    Send the link and the password through different channels for extra safety.

  3. 3

    Read it once

    The recipient opens the link, enters the password, and the memo decrypts in their browser.
