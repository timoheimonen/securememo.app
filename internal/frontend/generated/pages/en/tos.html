3. Service Description

securememo.app lets a user create an encrypted memo, share the memo URL with a recipient, and share the password separately. The recipient uses the password to decrypt the memo in their browser. Memos are deleted after successful read confirmation or expiration.

The Service is provided without user accounts. It is intended for temporary memo sharing, not permanent storage, backup, archiving, identity management, or emergency communication.

4. Encryption and Deletion Model

  • Memo content is encrypted in the browser before upload.
  • The memo password is generated and handled in the browser. The server does not receive or store it.
  • The server stores the encrypted memo payload, memo ID, expiration time, and deletion-token hash.
  • Expiration options are 8 hours, 24 hours, 48 hours, 1 week, and 30 days.
  • After successful decryption, the browser attempts to confirm deletion with the server.
  • Expired memos are removed by automatic cleanup jobs.
  • After deletion or expiration, the Service does not provide app-level recovery.
